Ingredients

  • 6 ½ (1-ounce) squares semisweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • ½ cup butter, cut into 8 pieces
  • 3 large eggs, room temperature
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder

Chocolate Lava Cake

A rich, gooey-centered chocolate dessert that melts in your mouth. Perfect for special occasions or a cozy night in. A chocolate lava cake (or molten chocolate cake) is a popular dessert that combines a rich, chocolate cake with a liquid, gooey center that oozes out like lava when cut. It is essentially a small cake with a fully baked, firm exterior and an underbaked, molten interior.

PREP TIME

15 mins

COOK TIME

35 mins

SERVINGS

4 People

SKILL LEVEL

Intermediate

Cooking Instructions

1

Prepare the chocolate

Finely chop the semisweet chocolate and set aside.

2

Melt the butter

Heat the butter gently until fully melted, then let it cool slightly.

3

Mix wet ingredients

Whisk the eggs, sugar, and vanilla until smooth and pale.
